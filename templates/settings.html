❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How do I change my profile picture?

Go to Settings → Account and upload a new avatar, or go to your Account page and use the Save Profile form.

How do I add music to my profile?

Go to Settings → Profile Music, search for a song, and click Select to set it as your profile clip.

How do I delete a post?

Tap the Edit button on your post, then use the Delete option. Only your own posts can be deleted.

Who can see my posts?

You control visibility when creating a post: Public, Followers, Close Friends, or Only Me. Change this in Settings → Privacy.

How do I report a problem?

Use the Send Feedback form above or email us at support@vybeflow.com. We take all reports seriously.
